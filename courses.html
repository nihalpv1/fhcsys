COURSE DESCRIPTION

This course focuses on the removal of nitrogenous waste products from the body through haemodialysis. Dialysis technologists play a critical role in the healthcare team by ensuring safe and hygienic dialysis treatments for patients.

Throughout the course, students will acquire knowledge and skills related to various procedures, equipment operations, and patient-handling techniques. Additionally, they will learn about the clinical aspects of dialysis treatment and the vital role of the dialysis technologist within a healthcare setting.

Specifically, the course will cover topics such as machine preparation, performing procedures under the guidance of a nephrologist, and monitoring patients throughout the dialysis process to ensure optimal care. By the end of the course, students will be equipped with the necessary skills to provide effective dialysis treatment as part of a multidisciplinary healthcare team.
