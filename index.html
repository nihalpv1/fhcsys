ABOUT US

Transforming healthcare education

At the FHC Institute of Paramedical Science, our passion lies in transforming lives through education and compassionate healthcare. Our mission is to empower the next generation of healthcare professionals to provide exceptional care and support to renal patients.

OUR VISION

We aim to be a leading institution in the renal healthcare community, recognized for our commitment to high-quality care that saves lives and reduces hospitalizations. With the support of esteemed parent organizations, Formen Healthcare and FHC Renal Care, we uphold a legacy of excellence in paramedical education and healthcare services.

  • Bachelor of Vocational Studies (BVoc) in Dialysis Technology – 3 Years

Welcome to the FHC Institute of Paramedical Science. We believe in the transformative power of education and are dedicated to improving the health and well-being of individuals undergoing dialysis treatment by providing comprehensive care and support services. As the Managing Director and principal of FHC Institute, I take pride in our commitment to shaping the next generation of healthcare professionals to care for and support renal patients with compassion.

Our vision is to become a leading institution in the renal healthcare community by prioritizing high-quality care that saves lives and reduces hospitalizations. We provide a variety of courses, including the Bachelor of Vocational Studies (BVoc) in Dialysis Technology, a Diploma in Advanced Dialysis Technology, and a Diploma in Dialysis. These programs are designed to equip our students with the knowledge and skills needed to become successful Dialysis Technologists. With the support of esteemed parent organizations such as Formen Healthcare and Astra Medicare, we maintain a strong reputation for excellence.

Our experienced faculty members are committed to helping you develop the skills and knowledge needed to excel in your career. As a student at FHC Institute, you will have access to state-of-the-art laboratory facilities and opportunities to interact with renowned Nephrologists. This will provide you with practical experience and insights into the latest dialysis technologies and procedures.

At FHC Institute, we recognize the importance of job placement and offer 100% job assistance to all our students. Additionally, we provide free training, loan facilities, and air-conditioned classrooms to create a conducive learning environment.

Education is the most powerful tool you can use to change the world. We invite you to join us at the FHC Institute of Paramedical Science, where you will receive the education and support necessary to make a real difference in the lives of renal patients.
